About Our Principal Madam

ORCHIDS - The International school School

Ms. Bidyunmala R Salunkel has been in the field of education for more than 27 years, with qualitative experience in teaching and administration. She holds an M.Sc degree with B.Ed. Her close association with Orchids has encouraged strategic thinking, action, and innovation of the teachers and students. She received the Women of Courage award from Gandhi Museum Barrackpore for uplifting women's education. She has also successfully conducted a lot of CBSE examinations as Centre Superintendent.
